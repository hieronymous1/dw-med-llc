Meet Dr. Wallis

Dr. Wallis brings a broad, culturally informed perspective to modern medical care. She is known for her exceptional listening skills and her ability to synthesize complex medical information into clear, actionable guidance. Dr. Wallis is a former residency faculty member, recipient of multiple teaching awards, and holds the distinction of **FAAFP (Fellow of the American Academy of Family Physicians). She is also moving forward with certification in Obesity Medicine, reflecting her commitment to evidence-based prevention and metabolic health.

Through Paradigm by DW Medical, Dr. Wallis provides physician-led concierge telemedicine focused on menopause and women’s health, longevity and preventive care, weight and metabolic health, and medical second opinions for patients in the U.S. and internationally.
